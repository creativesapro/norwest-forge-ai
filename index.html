Ethics & Risk Architecture

Audit FERPA exposure, bias risks, and mission-alignment gaps before a single new tool is deployed. Build your ethical AI principles with your team, not for them.

Governance Design

Establish clear decision-making structures: who approves new AI tools, who monitors data use, and who owns the division's AI narrative with students.

Address staff anxiety before it becomes resistance. Develop the internal and external narrative that positions your division as thoughtful innovators, not reluctant adopters.
